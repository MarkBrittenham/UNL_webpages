Text: Introduction to Smooth Manifolds, by John M. Lee (1st edition, Springer Verlag).


This course, as its name is meant to imply, is intended to introduce you to the theory, techniques, and applications of smooth manifolds; broadly speaking, this subject is known as differential topology. In basic outline we will follow the text; the specific topics covered will depend partly on student interest.
